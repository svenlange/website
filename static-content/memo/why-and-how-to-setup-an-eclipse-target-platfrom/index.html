Why and how to setup an Eclipse Target Platfrom

  • Cleanness. By default the target platform is the Eclipse IDE with its hundreds of plugins. Usually only a very small portion of those are needed for your application
  • Decoupling. If you setup your own target platform you are nomore depended on the IDE.
